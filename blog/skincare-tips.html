1. Daily Cleansing Routine

Skincare ka pehla step hai cleansing. Gaya mein pollution bahut hai, isliye face ko daily clean karna zaroori hai. Morning aur evening dono time face wash karo. Gentle cleanser use karo jo oil aur dirt remove kare bina skin ko dry na kare.

Tip: Neem aur aloe vera based cleanser try karo. Yeh natural ingredients skin ko soothe karte hain aur acne se bachate hain.

2. Weekly Exfoliation

Dead skin cells ko remove karne ke liye weekly exfoliation karo. Gaya ka dry weather skin ko rough bana deta hai, isliye exfoliation se skin smooth aur bright hoti hai. Chemical exfoliants use karo jo gentle hain.

Benefit: Pores clear hote hain aur makeup better lagta hai. Par over-exfoliation se bachao, warna skin irritated ho sakti hai.

3. Moisturize Regularly

Gaya mein humidity low hoti hai, isliye moisturizer essential hai. Lightweight moisturizer use karo jo skin ko hydrate kare bina greasy feel na de. Night time rich cream lagao.

Pro Tip: Hyaluronic acid wala moisturizer try karo. Yeh skin ko deeply hydrate karta hai aur wrinkles se bachata hai.

4. Sunscreen Protection

Gaya ka sun strong hai, isliye sunscreen daily lagana zaroori. SPF 30+ wala sunscreen use karo jo UVA aur UVB rays se protect kare. Makeup ke pehle lagao.

Why Important: Sun damage se pigmentation aur early aging hota hai. Glowing skin ke liye sun protection must hai.

5. Healthy Diet and Hydration

Skincare bahar se hi nahi, andar se bhi hota hai. Gaya mein fresh fruits aur vegetables khana. Daily 8-10 glasses water piyo. Antioxidants rich food jaise berries aur green tea include karo.

Benefit: Skin ko natural glow milta hai aur acne kam hota hai. Hydration se skin plump aur healthy rehti hai.

6. Sleep and Stress Management

Good sleep skin ke liye important hai. 7-8 hours sleep lo. Stress se skin dull ho jati hai, isliye yoga aur meditation karo. Gaya mein morning walks karo fresh air ke liye.

Tip: Before bed, screen time kam karo aur relaxing routine follow karo. Yeh dark circles aur puffiness kam karta hai.
